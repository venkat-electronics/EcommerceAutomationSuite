## 📖 Overview

Complete test automation framework for **E-Commerce websites** using **Selenium WebDriver** and **TestNG**. This framework automates the entire e-commerce workflow including user authentication, product search, shopping cart, checkout, payment, and order management.

## ✨ Features

### 🔐 User Authentication
- ✅ User Registration with validation
- ✅ Login with valid/invalid credentials
- ✅ Password recovery and reset
- ✅ Session management
- ✅ Remember me functionality
- ✅ Social media login (Google, Facebook)
- ✅ Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) testing
- ✅ Account lockout handling

### 🔍 Product Search & Navigation
- ✅ Basic and advanced search
- ✅ Auto-suggestions during typing
- ✅ Voice search testing
- ✅ Category browsing
- ✅ Filter by price, brand, rating, size, color
- ✅ Sort by price, popularity, rating
- ✅ Pagination handling
- ✅ Infinite scroll testing
- ✅ Search with Boolean operators (AND/OR/NOT)

### 📄 Product Details Page (PDP)
- ✅ Product information validation (title, price, description)
- ✅ Product images and zoom functionality
- ✅ 360-degree product view
- ✅ Product variants (size, color, configuration)
- ✅ Reviews and ratings
- ✅ Q&A section
- ✅ Product comparison
- ✅ Stock availability verification
- ✅ Delivery date estimation

### 🛒 Shopping Cart
- ✅ Add to cart
- ✅ Update quantity
- ✅ Remove items
- ✅ Save for later
- ✅ Move to wishlist
- ✅ Apply coupon codes
- ✅ Price calculations (subtotal, tax, discount, total)
- ✅ Multi-vendor cart support
- ✅ Cart persistence across sessions

### 💳 Checkout & Payment
- ✅ Address management (add, edit, delete)
- ✅ Payment method selection (Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, Net Banking, Wallet, COD)
- ✅ Card validation (number, expiry, CVV)
- ✅ EMI options
- ✅ Gift card and loyalty points
- ✅ Order confirmation
- ✅ Email and SMS notifications
- ✅ Invoice generation and download

### 📦 Order Management
- ✅ Order history
- ✅ Order status tracking
- ✅ Order cancellation
- ✅ Return and exchange processing
- ✅ Refund tracking
- ✅ Order filtering and sorting

### ❤️ Wishlist & Follow
- ✅ Add/Remove from wishlist
- ✅ Price drop alerts
- ✅ Back in stock alerts
- ✅ Multiple wishlists
- ✅ Share wishlist

### 📊 Reporting
- ✅ Extent HTML Reports with screenshots
- ✅ Allure Reports
- ✅ TestNG Reports
- ✅ Screenshots on failure
- ✅ Video recording (optional)
- ✅ Email notifications

### ⚡ Performance
- ✅ Page load time tracking
- ✅ Performance metrics
- ✅ Resource usage monitoring

### 🔄 CI/CD Integration
- ✅ Jenkins pipeline
- ✅ GitHub Actions
- ✅ Docker support
- ✅ Selenium Grid integration
